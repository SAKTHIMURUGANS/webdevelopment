Overview

The Prediction and Result page allows users to input patient data and receive predictions regarding liver disease. The system analyzes the provided information and classifies the condition based on trained machine learning models.

How to Get Results

To get the liver disease prediction result, follow these simple steps:

  • Input Data: Upload patient data in CSV format.
  • Preprocessing: The system will clean and normalize the data automatically.
  • Prediction: Our AI model will analyze the data and generate a result.
  • Result Output: The predicted result will classify whether the patient is at risk of liver disease or not.
