Introduction

Liver diseases are a major health concern, and early diagnosis is crucial for effective treatment. Our AI-based Liver Disease Prediction system leverages machine learning algorithms to analyze patient data and provide accurate predictions.

Key Featues

  1. Multi-Algorithm Approach – Uses Random Forest and KNN for improved accuracy.
  2. User-Friendly Interface – Simple and easy-to-use system for doctors and researchers.
  3. Real-Time Data Analysis – Enables quick and accurate predictions.
  4. Seamless Integration – API support for hospital management systems.

How it Works?

  • Data Collection – Patient health records in CSV format.
  • Preprocessing – Cleaning and normalizing data.
  • Model Execution – ML models analyze and classify the data.
  • Prediction Output – Displays liver disease classification results.

Why Choose Our System?

  • Increases diagnostic accuracy.
  • Helps doctors make data-driven decisions.
  • Reduces misdiagnosis risks.
