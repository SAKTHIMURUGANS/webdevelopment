Welcome to Liver Disease Prediction System

"An AI-powered solution for accurate and early liver disease detection."

Overview

Our Liver Disease Prediction Model is built using a well-structured dataset containing patient health records. The dataset plays a crucial role in training the machine learning algorithms to accurately classify and predict liver disease.

Liver Disease Attributes & Descriptions

Attribute Description
Age Patient's age in years.
Gender Male/Female classification.
Total Bilirubin Level of bilirubin in the blood.
Direct Bilirubin Direct form of bilirubin affecting liver function.
Alkaline Phosphatase (ALP) Enzyme level indicating liver damage.
Alanine Aminotransferase (ALT) Enzyme released due to liver inflammation.
Aspartate Aminotransferase (AST) High levels indicate liver disease.
Total Proteins Measures overall liver health.
Albumin Protein that helps in liver function.
Albumin & Globulin Ratio Ratio indicating liver disease severity.
Disease Condition Indicates whether the patient has liver disease (Yes/No).

Source of the Dataset

The dataset has been collected from medical institutions and healthcare research sources. It contains anonymized patient records, ensuring privacy and security.

Data Preprocessing Steps

  • Data Splitting: Dividing the dataset into training and testing sets for model evaluation.
  • Feature Selection: Identifying the most relevant attributes for prediction.
  • Normalization: Standardizing numerical values to ensure consistency.
  • Handling Missing Values: Any missing data points are filled or removed.

Why This Dataset?

  • Comprehensive Medical Data – Covers key liver function indicators.
  • Real-World Patient Records – Ensures model reliability.
  • Optimized for AI Models – Preprocessed for better accuracy.
